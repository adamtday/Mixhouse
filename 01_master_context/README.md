# `01_master_context/`

The strategic spine of MixHouse. Every other folder reduces back to a claim that lives here. If a file outside this folder contradicts something in here, the file outside is wrong.

## Reading Order

For a founder review, read in this order:

1. **`founder_thesis.md`** (confidential, owned by Sam Salony). The originating worldview, conviction, taste, and cultural insight behind MixHouse, in the founder's voice. **Start here.** This is the upstream input for everything else. Currently a template for Sam to complete.
2. **`mixhouse_master_thesis.md`** (investor). The strategic master argument: what it is, why it exists, why now, why it wins, why it becomes franchise IP. Derived from and accountable to the founder thesis.
3. **`audience_matrix.md`** (investor). The nine audiences, what each wants, fears, doubts, and requires as proof. The canonical input for every page brief and outreach lane.
4. **`objection_matrix.md`** (investor). The ten Universal Objections plus per-audience objection tables with response, proof, site routing, and supporting assets. The website is engineered as an objection-neutralisation engine; this file is the audit.
5. **`mixhouse_white_space.md`** (in `04_competitive_landscape/`). The primary investor-persuasion artefact. Three adjacent categories, the four-legs framing, why the gap has stayed open, what defends the position.
6. **`format_engine.md`** (in `02_show_bible/`). The recurring mechanic that makes MixHouse a format, not a show. Explains why the loop produces engagement, conflict, weekly return, viral clipping, contestant motivation, and escalating stakes.

For a quick read, the founder thesis and the master thesis alone carry the argument.

## File Roles

- **`founder_thesis.md`** (canonical, confidential). Sam's originating conviction. Template; Sam fills.
- **`supporting_operators.md`** (optional, confidential). Modular credibility layer for non-founder contributors. Optional add-on, not core to the argument. Does not compete with the founder thesis.
- **`mixhouse_master_thesis.md`** (canonical, investor). Strategic master argument.
- **`audience_matrix.md`** (canonical, investor). Nine-audience map.
- **`objection_matrix.md`** (canonical, investor). Per-audience objection tables.
- **`one_sentence_positioning.md`** (canonical, public-aware). Audience-variant one-liners; updates when the master thesis updates.
- **`glossary.md`** (canonical, internal). Controlled vocabulary; capitalised concepts and their definitions.

`04_competitive_landscape/mixhouse_white_space.md` and `02_show_bible/format_engine.md` are not in this folder but are part of the canonical spine; they sit in their topical folders for routing reasons.

## Authoring Rules

- **Founder vs operator distinction.** `founder_thesis.md` is Sam's voice and Sam's voice only. `supporting_operators.md` is everyone else, framed as supporting, not coequal, unless a contributor's coequal status is formally true and Sam has agreed in writing.
- **No biography in `founder_thesis.md`.** It is a worldview document, not a resume.
- **No public-copy paraphrasing of the founder thesis.** Public-facing language is derived through a deliberate translation pass that Sam reviews, not copied from the source document.
- **Updates here cascade.** Edits to the master thesis, audience matrix, objection matrix, or founder thesis trigger review of downstream page briefs and outputs.

## Phase Sequencing (Current)

- **Phase 1.** Canonical context layer. This folder, plus the white-space file and the format engine. The founder thesis template is the final piece for Sam to complete.
- **Phase 1.5.** Brand voice, revenue model, glossary refinement. Begins after Sam's founder thesis is reviewed and the canonical spine is locked.
- **Phase 2.** Evidence collation. Source the A/B-tier citations referenced as "target" in the Evidence rows across the canonical files. Lifts `assumption: true` file by file as rows are sourced.
- **Phase 3.** Page-brief refinement and downstream artefact generation. Does not begin before Phase 1 close and Sam's founder thesis review.

Supporting operators is optional and parallel; it can be filled in alongside any phase but is not blocking.
