# Production Risks

## Purpose

Catalogue the most consequential operational, creative, and commercial risks across the franchise — and the mitigations in place or required. The risk register is reviewed quarterly and at every major milestone gate.

## Key Takeaways

- Five categories of risk: creative, operational, commercial, rights, cultural.
- The most severe risks are not production logistics — they are cultural authenticity and music rights.
- Every risk has an owner, a mitigation, and a measurable monitoring signal.
- Risk register is a living document; entries are dated and reviewed.

## Core Content

### Risk Register

#### Creative Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Format engine fails to produce drama / character on camera | High | Tight episode anatomy; character-led casting; mentor-led creative pressure. | Showrunner. |
| Casting fails to deliver scene-credible ensemble | High | Triple-channel pipeline; advisor referrals. | Casting Director. |
| Music output below scene quality bar | High | Quality gates in pipeline; mentor caliber. | Music Supervisor. |
| Edit favours format mechanics over character | Medium | Format Co. approval on rough cuts. | Showrunner + Format Co. |

#### Operational Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Venue unavailability for `Drop Night` | Medium | Backup venues pre-secured. | Production Manager. |
| Weather / outdoor finale | Medium | Insurance + contingency venue. | Live Events Lead. |
| Cast withdrawal mid-shoot | Medium | Standby cast; insurance; format permits a pause. | Producer. |
| Crew availability in peak season | Medium | Pre-booking 12 months out. | Line Producer. |
| Tech failure during `Drop Night` | Medium | Redundant capture; safeguards in elimination logic. | Tech Lead. |

#### Commercial Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sponsor LOIs don't convert | Medium | Multi-category pipeline; conservative S1 underwriting. | Commercial Lead. |
| Broadcaster pulls greenlight post-pilot | High | Pilot independently financed; alternative streamer paths. | Format Co. CEO. |
| First territory under-delivers ratings | High | Multi-line revenue (music + live) doesn't depend on linear ratings. | Format Co. CEO. |
| Format-sale market stagnates | Medium | Hold flagship territory revenue; focus on platform extensions. | Commercial Lead. |

#### Rights and Legal Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Talent disputes royalty splits | High | Transparent rights deal pre-cast; legal documentation. | Legal + Music Supervisor. |
| Sync clearance issues on samples | Medium | Sample clearance owned by Music Supervision; original-only floor. | Music Supervisor. |
| Label partner conflicts with central Music Supervision | Medium | Clear decision-rights map; central Format Co. approval. | Format Co. |
| Format infringement claim | Medium | Format bible documented, dated, and IP-registered. | Legal. |

#### Cultural Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Scene rejects the show as inauthentic | **Critical** | Advisor governance; anti-pattern enforcement; scene-credible judges. | Format Co. + Brand Lead. |
| Talent walkout due to edit / treatment | High | No villain edits; transparent edits; mental-health support. | Showrunner. |
| Press treats format as cringe | Medium | Pre-launch scene-press relationships; substantive press kit. | Communications Lead. |
| Cultural appropriation in sub-genre representation | High | Sub-genre advisors and consultants; diverse judging panel. | Brand Lead. |

### Monitoring Signals

- Casting funnel weekly KPI: applications + referral quality.
- Music pipeline weekly KPI: tracks shipped, QC pass rate.
- Sponsor pipeline weekly KPI: LOIs in flight.
- Cultural signal: scene-press monitoring, advisor sentiment, contestant testimony.

### Escalation Process

- Owner identifies risk activation.
- Mitigation is triggered immediately.
- Risk register updated within 24h.
- Format Co. leadership briefed weekly on active escalations.

### Critical Path Risks

The two risks that, if they activate, jeopardise the franchise:

1. **Scene rejection** — addressed by `07_brand_world/cultural_authenticity.md` and advisor governance.
2. **Music rights blow-up** — addressed by `05_business_model/music_rights_strategy.md` and per-contestant transparent contracting.

These get disproportionate ongoing attention.
