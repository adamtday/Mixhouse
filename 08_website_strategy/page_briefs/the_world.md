# The World Page Brief

## Purpose

Define the page that conveys what MixHouse looks, sounds, and feels like — the brand world — without explaining the format. This is the page that closes the credibility gap with the scene.

## Key Takeaways

- L2 (Story).
- The most image- and motion-led page on the site.
- Communicates location, look, advisors-light, and cultural posture.
- The page that the scene shares.

## Core Content

### Disclosure Layer

L2 (Story), with strong atmospheric L1 reinforcement.

### Page Outline

1. **Hero — The place.**
   - Looping aerial / location cinematic. One editorial line.
2. **Visual language.**
   - Media carousel of moodboard references: residency, club, festival, faces.
3. **The voices behind the room.**
   - Advisor preview strip (3–6 names) with link to Advisors page.
4. **What we sound like.**
   - Audio component — a curated short mix or pre-show sound piece.
5. **What we will not be.**
   - Quiet public-facing statement of cultural posture. Distilled from `07_brand_world/cultural_authenticity.md` and `07_brand_world/anti_patterns.md`. Carefully written; quiet, not pious.
6. **Where it lives.**
   - Light location reveal (or tease, depending on lock status). Source: `06_production_strategy/location_strategy.md`.
7. **The press kit.**
   - Public assets link + Materials gate link.

### Audience Priority

| Order | Audience | What they get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Fan / scene | Cinematic confidence; cultural respect signal. |
| 2 | Advisor | Brand world they would attach to. |
| 3 | Press / scene press | Quotable cultural posture. |

### Tone

- Cinematic, editorial.
- No marketing.
- Some quiet courage in the cultural posture statement.

### Cultural Posture Statement (working draft)

> "Twelve producers. The room they play. The label that releases them. The festival that headlines them. Real, the whole way down. No villain edits. No covers. No tourists."

This is the public surface of the anti-pattern discipline.

### Components and Animation Notes

- Hero looping clip: longer than home hero (8–12s allowed).
- Motion is slow, deliberate.
- Audio component requires explicit user start (no autoplay audio).

### Performance and Media Targets

- LCP < 2.5s.
- AudioPlayer lazy-loaded.
- Cinematic carousel uses AVIF / WebP with optimised dimensions.
