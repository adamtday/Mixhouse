# Contact Page Brief

## Purpose

Define the public inbound surface — a single page that routes every kind of inquiry to the right MixHouse owner.

## Key Takeaways

- L2 (Story).
- One form, audience-segmented routing.
- Quiet, editorial, low-friction.
- Auto-acknowledge; manual response within defined SLA.

## Core Content

### Disclosure Layer

L2 (Story).

### Page Outline

1. **Editorial intro.**
   - One short paragraph: "Tell us who you are and what you're working on."
2. **The form.**
   - Required: name, email, inquiry type, message.
   - Inquiry type dropdown: Press · Talent / casting · Advisor inquiry · Investor / broadcaster / sponsor (routes to Materials) · Other.
3. **Direct routes (optional).**
   - Press inbox.
   - Casting CTA (full form).
   - Materials gate.

### Audience Priority

| Order | Audience | What they get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | All inbound | Clear routing to the right team. |
| 2 | Press | Faster path with direct contact. |
| 3 | Talent | Path to casting submission. |

### Tone

- Editorial, brief, kind.
- No "we'd love to hear from you" filler.
- No marketing language.

### Components Used

- EditorialSpread
- ContactForm (segmented)
- Direct-route CTAs

### Routing Logic

| Inquiry type | Destination |
| --- | --- |
| Press | Press inbox; auto-tag for EPK delivery if requested. |
| Talent / casting | Routes to full casting CTA on The Show. |
| Advisor | Advisor inquiry inbox. |
| Investor / broadcaster / sponsor | Routes to Materials gate with audience pre-selected. |
| Other | General inbox; manual triage. |

### Auto-Acknowledgement

- All submissions receive an auto-acknowledgement email immediately.
- Manual responses target within 5 business days.

### Performance and Media Targets

- LCP < 2.5s.
- Zero motion required.

## Objections Answered

| Objection | Response |
| --- | --- |
| "Just put an email address." | An audience-segmented form filters and routes faster than a shared inbox. |
| "Five days is too slow." | It's the realistic SLA. Beating expectation > missing it. |

## Website Implications

- Lowest visual investment of the seven pages, highest operational reliability requirement.
- The form is the make-or-break user experience here.
