# Apple TV+ Marketing Page

## Observed Page Content

Modular vertically-stacked layout: hero section, subscription block, sports offerings, device compatibility, FAQs, persistent top navigation. Hero leads with the service name and tagline "All Apple Originals. Streaming only on Apple TV." Two CTAs ("Stream now", "Try it free") frame the messaging. Formula 1 imagery anchors the hero, establishing aspirational content credibility.

Large sans-serif headlines dominate sections. Supporting body copy in smaller weights. Secondary headings organise content into chunks. Visual hierarchy prioritises service benefits over specific titles.

No film-specific artwork dominates. Branded partnership logos (F1, MLS, MLB) signal content breadth without showing individual show tiles. Shows and films are abstracted into genre descriptors and partnership badges rather than visual galleries.

Sample editorial line: "Thrilling dramas, epic sci-fi, and feel-good comedies."

## Mechanism Analysis

This page makes a notable choice: it does not tile shows. The mechanism:

1. **Partnership-as-credibility framing.** Formula 1, MLS, and MLB logos do credibility work that an individual-show tile-grid cannot. Audiences read partnership scale as a quality signal.
2. **Service-attribute lead over title lead.** The page sells the service rather than the catalogue. This is the opposite of Netflix's home page, which leads with title artwork.
3. **Restraint on the catalogue surface.** By not showing show artwork, the page sustains a single brand-world register rather than mosaic-of-show-art chaos.

## What Should Translate To MixHouse

- Partnership-as-credibility pattern when MixHouse partners with festivals, labels, or DSPs (named partnerships in the visual layer carry credibility that copy cannot).
- Service-attribute lead pattern for the home page: lead with the world MixHouse builds, not with the cycle catalogue.
- Restraint on the catalogue surface to sustain one brand-world register.

## What Should NOT Translate To MixHouse

- The subscription-bundle framing as the primary commercial layer (MixHouse is a media franchise, not a subscription service).
- The device-compatibility list pattern (not relevant to MixHouse public surfaces).
- The "All Apple Originals" exclusivity framing (MixHouse operates across DSPs and broadcaster surfaces; exclusivity is not the message).
