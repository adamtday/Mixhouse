# Cirque du Soleil Homepage

## Observed Page Content

Leads with aspirational mission statement: "Open your world to more wonder. Feel the inspiration like never before." A transparent character illustration anchors the visual experience, emphasising spectacle over specific show promotion.

Architecture prioritises brand philosophy over transactional content. The headline "Welcome to a new era of Cirque du Soleil" establishes tone before any show information. Navigation architecture separates corporate functions (careers, sustainability) from entertainment discovery.

Inspirational, abstract copy register: "Entertainment that defies conformity". Typography hierarchy uses size differentials to move from philosophical statements downward to practical navigation.

Notably absent on home page: specific show listings by location, name, or venue. Page references "Cirque's Latest Show News & Articles" without displaying them; shows are discovered through secondary navigation rather than home-page real estate.

Footer reveals corporate complexity: distinct sections for corporate information, career opportunities, and affiliated entertainment brands (Blue Man Group, VStar Entertainment). Global partners receive prominent visual treatment.

Photography uses transparency effects, prioritising artistic impression over documentary realism. Approach emphasises wonder and imagination over literal venue or performance documentation.

## Mechanism Analysis

Cirque's home page demonstrates a brand-world-first commercial approach:

1. **Brand philosophy precedes show inventory.** The home page does not sell shows. It establishes the brand world that the shows are inside. This is a confident commercial choice that bets on brand-world retention over immediate ticketing.
2. **Transparent / illustrative imagery rather than photography.** Cirque chooses illustrative or transparency-effect imagery on the home page rather than performance photography. The choice signals art-direction continuity across many shows; no single show owns the home page.
3. **Affiliated-brands surfacing in the footer.** Blue Man Group and VStar Entertainment appear in the footer, building portfolio breadth as a credibility signal.

## What Should Translate To MixHouse

- Brand-philosophy-precedes-inventory pattern for the home page: lead with the world MixHouse builds, not with the cycle catalogue or finale dates.
- No-single-cycle-owns-the-home-page discipline: the home page operates above any specific cycle.
- Affiliated-property footer credibility pattern when MixHouse partners with festivals, labels, or platforms whose names carry weight.

## What Should NOT Translate To MixHouse

- The aspirational-abstract-copy register ("Open your world to more wonder"); this is Cirque's mid-tier corporate voice and reads as marketing-pace. MixHouse copy register is editorial-restrained.
- The transparent-character-illustration imagery pattern (Cirque uses illustration to manage many-shows continuity; MixHouse is single-franchise and uses photography).
- The corporate-careers-and-sustainability navigation prominence (MixHouse navigation is editorial, not corporate-functional).
