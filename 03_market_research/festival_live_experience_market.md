# Festival & Live Experience Market

## Purpose

Size the global live electronic-music and festival economy and identify the partner / venue / city ecosystem MixHouse must engage to deliver the `Drop Night` and `Final Set` mechanics credibly and economically.

## Key Takeaways

- Live music is the largest revenue segment in global music; electronic events account for a disproportionate share of festival ticket spend.
- The top 50 electronic-anchored festivals reach multi-million annual attendance.
- Demand exceeds supply at the top of the festival market; partnerships are gettable.
- Venue / city partnerships are a strategic lever for both production economics and territory adaptation.

### Festival Partner Categories

For MixHouse, the relevant partner types are:

| Partner type | Role | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Headline festival | `Final Set` host | Tomorrowland, Ultra, Coachella, EDC, Awakenings, Movement, Sónar. |
| Boutique / scene festival | Mid-season off-site | Dekmantel, Field Day, DGTL, Lost Village, Hideout. |
| Multi-city promoter | Tour delivery | Live Nation Electronic, AEG, Insomniac, Mixmag Live. |
| Resident club / superclub | `Drop Night` cinematic venue | Berghain, fabric, Hï/Ushuaïa, Output legacy, Womb, KOKO. |
| Livestream platform | Cinematic-live grammar | Cercle, Boiler Room, Resident Advisor. |

### Cities to Consider for Residency

- **Ibiza**: tier-1 brand, peak summer demand, infrastructure mature.
- **Berlin**: credibility, year-round scene, lower production cost.
- **Mexico City / Tulum**: emerging tentpole, scene momentum, cost-effective.
- **Amsterdam**: ADE ecosystem, scene depth.
- **Lisbon**: emerging, lower cost, strong producer community.
- **Athens / Mykonos**: visual identity, premium positioning.

See `06_production_strategy/location_strategy.md` for the full evaluation.

### Demand-Supply Dynamics

- Top-tier festivals sell out within minutes; secondary markets demand-constrained too.
- Brand partnerships at festivals are saturated; show-of-substance content stands out.
- Residency season (Ibiza, Mykonos) has the operational density to host a residency at scale.
- Winter scene (Amsterdam ADE, Berlin Atonal, Miami WMC) provides off-season touchpoints.

### Strategic Implications for the Format

- Headline festival partnership locked before season ordering, anchors the `Final Set` as a real, dated, marketable event.
- Multiple club partners across the season provide rotating `Drop Night` venues and a brand-safe variety pack.
- Livestream platform partner gives the cinematic-live execution muscle from day one.

### Live Economics That Compound

- Finalists' post-season tour at boutique venues.
- Anniversary `Drop Night` cinematic livestreams.
- Branded MixHouse live activations year-round.
- International format adaptations launch with a local festival anchor.
