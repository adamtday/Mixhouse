# Moodboard References

## Purpose

Anchor the cinematic and visual references that define MixHouse's look. The moodboard is not aspirational mood — it's a binding list of references that production, design, and marketing align against.

## Key Takeaways

- References are categorised by surface (show, web, social, live).
- Each reference is dated and stored in `10_source_material/references/` once images are captured.
- Anti-references (what we are deliberately not) are listed in `07_brand_world/anti_patterns.md`.
- The moodboard is a living artifact and changes as the brand world is locked.
